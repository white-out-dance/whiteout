Whiteout Setup

Play Requests

Whiteout manages the queue and links. It does not download Apple Music tracks or use Apple Music account cookies.

  1. Connect Whiteout to your room.
  2. In Stage or Overlay, click Open Link for the next request.
  3. Play it in Apple Music or your DJ software (if supported).
  4. Tap Played or Skip to keep the list clean.
